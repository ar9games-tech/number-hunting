Add a Punishment Card feature to Online Multiplayer in Number Hunt.

Feature location:
- Add a “Punishment” button inside the multiplayer room/game screen.
- This feature appears only when the room has 4 or more players.
- If the room has fewer than 4 players, hide the Punishment button.

Punishment feature concept:
- When players are playing as a group of 4 or more, they can tap “Punishment” to reveal a random punishment card.
- The reveal should look exciting like opening a card pack / mystery pack.

Punishment card reveal animation:
- Show a dramatic pack opening animation.
- Add glowing effects.
- Add shaking card pack effect.
- Add sparkle/particle effects.
- Add suspense delay before revealing the card.
- Add sound placeholder for pack opening.
- Add final reveal animation with zoom and glow.

Punishment cards:
1. Direct Elimination
   - Arabic: خروج مباشر
   - Effect: The selected player is eliminated immediately.

2. Vote
   - Arabic: تصويت
   - Effect: Players vote on the punishment result.

3. Cartoon Sandal Card
   - Arabic title: زنوبة كرتونية
   - Text under card:
     - Arabic: إذا لم يقبل العقاب، خروج مباشر
     - English: If the player refuses the punishment, direct elimination
   - Use a funny cartoon sandal illustration style.
   - Do not use real violence or offensive visuals.

4. Animal Sound Imitation
   - Arabic title: تقليد صوت حيوان
   - Text under card:
     - Arabic: إذا لم يقبل العقاب، خروج مباشر
     - English: If the player refuses the punishment, direct elimination
   - The player must imitate an animal sound.

UI requirements:
- Support Arabic and English.
- Arabic text must be RTL.
- Card titles should be large and clear.
- Punishment card screen should feel fun, not aggressive.
- Use family-friendly cartoon style.
- Add “Accept Punishment” button.
- Add “Refuse” button.
- If Refuse is tapped, show “Direct Elimination”.

Multiplayer rules:
- Punishment button only available in rooms with 4+ players.
- Server should broadcast the revealed punishment card to all players.
- All players should see the same revealed card.
- Prevent multiple players from opening punishment cards at the same time.
- Add cooldown after opening a punishment card.

Files/components to add:
- src/components/PunishmentButton.js
- src/components/PunishmentCardModal.js
- src/data/punishmentCards.js
- Add sound placeholder in src/services/soundManager.js

Server updates:
- Add Socket.io event:
  - requestPunishmentCard
  - punishmentCardRevealed
  - punishmentCardError
- Validate:
  - Room exists
  - Room has at least 4 players
  - Game is active
  - No punishment card is currently opening
- Randomly select one punishment card and broadcast it to the room.

Important:
- Keep the feature optional and fun.
- Do not break normal multiplayer guessing.
- Do not show Punishment button in Solo Mode.
- Do not show Punishment button in 2-player or 3-player rooms.
