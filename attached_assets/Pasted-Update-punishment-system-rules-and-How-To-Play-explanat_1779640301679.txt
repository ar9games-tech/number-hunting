English:

Punishments are available only in sessions with 3 or more players.

3 Players:
- Final Elimination
- Forgiveness
- Choose Another Player

4+ Players:
- Final Elimination
- Forgiveness
- Vote
- Choose Another Player

Punishment explanations:

Final Elimination:
The player is removed immediately.

Forgiveness:
The player is forgiven and the next round starts.

Vote:
Players vote on whether the punished player should stay or be eliminated.

Choose Another Player:
The punished player chooses another player to receive the punishment instead, then presses the Punishment button to reveal a new punishment.

Notes:
- Punishments appear only after a match ends.
- Only the winner can start a punishment.
- Random Match does not include punishments.
- Next rounds start automatically depending on punishment results.
