## Description (4,000 chars max)

Number Hunting is the fast, addictive party game where every guess is a hunt.

Pick a secret number. Take turns guessing. Beat your friends to the target, score the most points, and crown a winner — all in matches that finish in just a few minutes.

▸ **Online multiplayer.** Create a private room with a 4-letter code or join a random match. Play with friends in the next chair or across the world — no account needed.

▸ **Local pass-and-play.** Hand the phone around the table. Perfect for road trips, dinner parties, and waiting rooms.

▸ **Voting & punishments.** When the round ends, vote on who has to take the funny consequence. Built-in punishments range from silly to savage, and you can write your own.

▸ **Quick matches.** Most games finish in 3–5 minutes. Designed for short attention spans and long laughs.

▸ **Bilingual.** Full English and Arabic interfaces, with RTL layout support.

▸ **Privacy-first.** No accounts. No data collection. Game state lives in memory and disappears when the match ends.

▸ **Free to play.** Supported by ads — never during gameplay, only between matches.

Whether you're killing time with one friend or hosting a party of eight, Number Hunting turns simple math into pure social chaos. Download free, pick a room code, and let the hunt begin.

## Review Notes (Notes for the Reviewer)
Paste this in the "App Review Information → Notes" field:

> Number Hunting is a casual party game with no login or account system. To test online multiplayer, please:
>
> 1. Tap "Online" on the home screen.
> 2. Tap "Create Room" to generate a 4-letter room code, OR tap "Quick Match" for random matchmaking.
> 3. On a second device or simulator, repeat step 1 and tap "Join Room" with the code from step 1.
> 4. Both players can now play through a full match.
>
> The game also has a fully offline "Local Game" mode if you only have one device available — pass the phone between players.
>
> Ads (Google AdMob test units in this build) appear only between matches, never during gameplay. The App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown on first launch.
