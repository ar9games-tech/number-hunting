Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 28, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Number Hunting ("we", "our", "the app") handles information when you use the iOS application.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Number Hunting does not require an account, does not ask for your name, email address, phone number, photos, contacts, location, or any other personal information to play the game. There is no login system.

2. Information We Process to Run the Game

To enable online multiplayer, we process the following temporarily in memory on our game server:

  • A randomly generated player ID (not linked to your real identity)
  • The nickname you choose for a given room (you can change it any time)
  • Your in-game actions (guesses, votes, punishments) during the match
  • A short-lived room code so you can rejoin a disconnected match

This data lives only for the duration of the match and is erased shortly after the room is closed. We do not write it to any long-term database, and we do not sell, share, or analyze it.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

The app shows ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect and process the following to serve ads:

  • Apple Advertising Identifier (IDFA) — only if you grant tracking permission
  • Approximate location derived from IP address
  • Device type and operating system version
  • Basic crash and performance diagnostics

You will see Apple's standard App Tracking Transparency prompt the first time you open the app. If you choose "Ask App Not To Track", we will instruct AdMob to serve non-personalized ads only.

Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy and the How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services page.

4. Data Stored on Your Device

The app stores the following only on your iPhone using standard iOS user defaults storage:

  • Your language preference and other in-app settings
  • Your last-used nickname (so you don't have to retype it)
  • Whether you have already seen tutorial screens

This information never leaves your device. Uninstalling the app erases it permanently.

5. Children's Privacy

Number Hunting is rated for general audiences but is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with information, contact us and we will remove it.

6. Your Rights

Because we do not store personal information on a long-term basis, there is generally nothing about you for us to delete, export, or correct. If you have questions or wish to make a privacy request, contact us at the email below and we will respond within 30 days.

7. Changes to This Policy

If we change this Privacy Policy, we will update the "Effective date" above and post the new version at this URL. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions? Email ar9games@gmail.com.
